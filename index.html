Our Services

અમે શું Jobwork કરીએ છીએ?

Saree, blouse, lehenga – tamara kapda par amaro haath lagse to ek alag hi chamak aavse. Amari specialties:

🪡

Machine Embroidery

High-quality computerized embroidery with precision threading on georgette, chiffon, net, and silk sarees.

Sequin & Stone Work

Hand and machine sequin placement, stone setting, kundan detailing for bridal and party wear sarees.

Zari & Thread Work

Bulk Jobwork
